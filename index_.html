ABOUT US

Far from the bustling city life, Ramada Almukalla Hotel is a real holiday treat. It is situated 29 km from the city airport "Alrayan airport" and adjacent to the famous Arabian sea.
Ramada Almukalla Hotel is the haven for family members, tourists, travel enthusiasts and businessmen who want to get away from work for a while. For the frequent shoppers, it's a delight to know that Ramada Almukalla Hotel is near from traditional souks and modern commercial centers situated just 10 km away.
Come and stay at Ramada Almukalla Hotel enjoy the many things we have to offer :
- 106 rooms and 6 suites in 5 floors
- Non-smoking bedrooms
- Granada restaurant
- Andalus hall
- coffee shop
- Pool bar
- Lobby coffee corner
and more...
